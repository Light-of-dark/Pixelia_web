Overview

Engine: Developed in Godot using GDScript.

Currencies:

  • Standard Currency: Pixibucks
  • Premium Currency: Pixigold

Classes

Knight

  • Crusader: Specializes in holy damage and self-healing.
  • Dark Knight: Uses life-draining attacks and curses.
  • Dragoon: Increased mobility with powerful leaping attacks.
  • Blade Master: Dual swords for faster attacks and combos.

Mage

  • Elementalist: Masters fire, ice, lightning with area-of-effect spells.
  • Necromancer: Summons undead creatures and drains life.
  • Time Weaver: Manipulates time to slow enemies and speed up allies.
  • Arcane Scholar: Buffs spells and magical traps.

Assassin

  • Shadow Stalker: Stealth and backstabbing.
  • Poisoner: Uses poisons and toxins.
  • Nightblade: Combines melee and shadow magic for burst damage.
  • Saboteur: Sets traps and bombs.

Healer

  • Cleric: Strong defensive and resurrection abilities.
  • Druid: Nature-based healing and temporary beasts.
  • Blood Healer: Uses own health to heal others.
  • Aura Priest: Healing through auras and buffs.
  • Spirit Walker: Communicates with spirits for healing and curses.

Tank

  • Juggernaut: Strength and damage absorption.
  • Shield Bearer: Blocking and deflecting attacks.
  • Ironclad: Heavy armor and debuff conversion.
  • War Golem: Mechanical tank with self-repair and modern attacks.

Lore

The Forbidden Legend of the Demon Lord

Long ago, the Demon Lord Akuma ruled over the dark dimension known as Oblivion's Rift. His essence was scattered across 10 forbidden gems, each holding a fragment of his soul. These were hidden in places beyond mortal reach.

The Cult of the Demon Lord and the Defeat of the Nine Kingdoms

The Cult of the Demon Lord, led by Grandmaster Malakar, sought these gems to resurrect Akuma. They conquered 9 great kingdoms, claiming the gems hidden within. The 10th gem, however, was hidden so well that it remained a mystery.

The Call to Arms: Adventurers Against the Cult

As the cult grew stronger, kingdoms sought heroes to stop them. The cultâ€™s stronghold was in the Land of the Demons, where they manipulated the local demon community. Adventurers, including you, invaded this land to thwart their plans.

The Invasion of the Demon Land

The cult had corrupted both the land and its inhabitants. Adventurers battled through demonic forces and cultists, seeking to prevent the cult from uncovering the final gem and completing their ritual.

The Betrayal of Akuma

The cult succeeded in finding the 10th gem and resurrecting Akuma. However, the Demon Lord betrayed them, killing the cult members and taking over the world.

The Final Battle

You, the last hope, must face Akuma. This final battle will determine the fate of Pixelia. Only by unlocking ancient powers can you hope to defeat the Demon Lord and end his reign.
